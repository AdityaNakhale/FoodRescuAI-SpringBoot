About Food Rescue AI

At Food Rescue AI, we believe that hunger is a logistics problem, not a resource problem. Every year, trillions of dollars worth of food go to waste while millions go hungry. Our mission is to bridge this gap through advanced artificial intelligence.

Developed by JK Innovative Private Limited, our platform uses machine learning to analyze surplus data, optimize delivery routes, and ensure that food reaches its destination before it expires. We empower restaurants, grocery stores, and event centers to turn their waste into impact.
